Basic Idea of the System

A system that provides real time emergency service vehicle tracking and a definite and clear path to the emergency service vehicles while alerting the nearest Traffic Police organs enroute to monitor and clear the traffic as situation requires. As soon as the vehicle is about to begin the journey, the driver will start the navigation, enter the destination and the system will display the optimal route. Once the driver starts navigating, all the details would be forwarded to the Police Control Room and the workstations enroute. The Police Control Room can track and monitor all the running systems and change the route in case the traffic at certain points are too congested to be cleared in minutes. The agents can track the vehicles that are approaching their workstations and decide the right course of action locally.

Reason for Developing

In the present art, the invention related to facilitating quick passage of a vehicle through a transportation system are known to have following disadvantages:

  • They require complex infrastructure supporting services.
  • Increased technical dependency.
  • Requires highly skilled workforce.

Thus there is a need to address the above mentioned disadvantages. The present invention aims to address the above mentioned disadvantages.
